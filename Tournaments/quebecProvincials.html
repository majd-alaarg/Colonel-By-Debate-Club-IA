Quebéc's Provincial British Parliamentary Tournament

We are pleased to invite you to the QSDA British Parliamentary Tournament which we are pleased to host at Selwyn House School on: Saturday, October 30th 2021

This tournament will serve as the Quebec qualifier for the Canadian National British Parliamentary Championship taking place online this year. There are junior and senior qualifying spaces, however, junior and senior teams will be debating against each other in this tournament, and qualifying order of junior teams will be determined after the tournament. All rules for qualifying teams (such as school caps and debating with your qualifying partner) can be found in the QSDA bylaws.
There will be four impromptu British Parliamentary rounds (with 5-minute speaking times), followed by a single, final, open round in the afternoon. Resources for British Parliamentary debating can be found at qsda.org under “Debating Resources”.

The registration deadline for this event is October 20th. Because of the complexities of running a BP tournament, the cap for the tournament is 40 teams. There is no registration fee for the tournament.
All schools are required to send at least one judge for the duration of the tournament. Additional judges are also helpful. Schools that wish to send more than four teams may be required to bring additional judges. The form to register will be sent upon emailing bracewellj@selwyn.ca.
Finally, as per the tentative schedule, please note that registration/check-in closes at 9:00 am on Sunday, February 6, 2022. We will unfortunately have to drop teams who are late.
