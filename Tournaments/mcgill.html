McGill's Annual British Parliamentary Debate

On behalf of the McGill Debating Union we are pleased to invite you to our upcoming 61st annual High School Debating Championships.

The tournament will consist of five rounds of British Parliamentary impromptu debate, four rounds on Saturday and one round on Sunday, breaking to semi-finals, junior finals, and finals. This tournament will not include a prepared round. There will be seminars offered on a variety of topics on Friday prior to the tournament.

Registration will be open on September 27th, 2021 at precisely 4:00pm EST and will be conducted on a first-come, first-served basis. The deadline to register is October 12, 2021 at midnight. We recommend that you register as soon as possible as spots fill up quickly. Schools are permitted to register up to five teams initially, and may submit additional teams who will be placed on the waiting list.
The form to register can be found here.
We will try to accommodate as many teams as possible. Following this intimation, we request that you send names of the debaters attending the tournament by Friday, October 15, 2021. This information is to be submitted via a google form which will be sent to institutions which successfully reserve spots.
Should there be any questions related to registration, payments, or the tournament, please do not hesitate contacting us on this email: mcgilldebating.highschool@gmail.com
