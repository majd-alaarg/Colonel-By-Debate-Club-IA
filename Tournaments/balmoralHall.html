Balmoral Hall’s 2021 “Rupert’s Land” Tournament

Balmoral Hall School invites you to the annual Rupert's Land Debate and Public Speaking Tournament for Grade 10 to Grade 12 students. This year, the tournament will be hosted virtually in two separate parts. The persuasive speech event will be held on the evening on Wednesday, December 8th (5:30 PM – 9:15 PM), while the impromptu debating event will be held on Sunday, December 12th (11:30 AM – 4:00 PM.

The top 4 speakers from the tournament will represent Manitoba at the National Public Speaking Championships which will be hosted by St. John’s Ravenscourt. Overall individual scores will be determined by combining the scores from the two rounds of persuasive speaking and the two rounds of debate.


Schools must submit the name of one judge for every 2 debaters for each day of the tournament, both the persuasive speeches on December 8th and the impromptu debates on December 12th. The same judge is welcome to judge both parts of the tournament If you are unable to bring a judge, we CANNOT guarantee that your team will compete.
