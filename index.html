The Colonel By Debate Club
Learn More

Fostering an environment of personal growth and debate knowledge for generations to come!

Colonel By Debate Club is ‘Arguably the Best’ club at Colonel By. Debate club helps you develop skills, from rhetorical strategies, to public speaking and confidence. Our weekly club hosts British Parliamentary debate rounds that cover relevant and specialized topics.

Our club is open to first-timers, as well as experienced debaters. Your first debate can often be intimidating, but our club fosters a welcoming and friendly environment.

Upcoming regional, provincial and national debate tournaments.

McGill British Parliamentary Tournament

Oct. 22, 2021 Discord/Zoom call

The tournament will consist of five rounds of British Parliamentary impromptu debate, four rounds on Saturday and one round on Sunday, breaking to semi-finals, junior finals, and finals. This tournament will not include a prepared round. There will be seminars offered on a variety of topics on Friday prior to the tournament.

Carleton's Annual Xerxes Cup

Nov. 19-20, 2021 Discord

The tournament will consist of five rounds of British Parliamentary impromptu debate. There will be no prepared resolutions. There will be two rounds Friday night and three rounds Saturday, breaking to Open semi-finals and Junior finals. This tournament will take place entirely ONLINE using Discord as a platform.

OSDU Provincial Tournament

Oct. 30, 2021 Zoom

The tournament will consist of four rounds of British Parliamentary impromptu debate, three rounds on Saturday and one round on Sunday, breaking to semi-finals, junior finals, and senior finals. This tournament will qualify debaters to attend the Canadian National British Parliamentary Championships, to take place online in late November, 2021.

McGill's Winter Bonanza Tournament

Jan. 29, 2022 McGill Montreal Campus

The tournament will consist of six rounds of Canadian Parliamentary (CP) impromptu debate. There is no prepared resolution. There will be two rounds on Friday night, and four rounds on Saturday. The sixth round will be a hidden-quarter final and the tournament will break to open semi-finals.

Queen’s University National Debating Championship

Jan. 22, 2022 Discord

The Queen’s Debating Union is the oldest debate club in Canada, having been established in 1843. This tournament will provide you with 5 high-quality and accessible rounds of preliminary debate in British Parliamentary style with 5 minute speaking times. The tournament will consist of 5 rounds of British Parliamentary Debate before the elimination rounds.

Peel and York’s 2021 Regional Debate Tournament

Feb. 6, 2022 TBD

This tournament is the qualifying event for the Senior Provincials, which will take place online in late February. For all rounds, students will debate in the Canadian National Debate Format (CNDF). For the first two rounds, each team will debate both sides of the Prepared resolution, and the third and fourth rounds will be Impromptu debating.

This tournament will serve as the Quebec qualifier for the Canadian National British Parliamentary Championship taking place online from November 19th to December 21st of this year. Dec. 8, 2021 Zoom
